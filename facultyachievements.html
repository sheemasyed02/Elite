Dr G. Murali

We are delighted to highlight the exceptional achievements of Dr G. Murali, the esteemed Head of our Artificial Intelligence Department. Dr. Murali's dedication to research and innovation has significantly contributed to the growth and reputation of our institution.
Key Achievements:
Groundbreaking Research: Dr Murali has made significant strides in the field of Artificial Intelligence with his groundbreaking research. His work has been recognized internationally, with publications in prestigious journals such as BEEI, IIETA, and Springer.
Pioneering Research in Stock Market Analysis: His research on stock market analysis using machine learning and deep learning algorithms has opened new avenues for investors and financial analysts.
Innovative Application of Neural Networks: Dr. Murali's innovative application of neural networks for sentimental analysis and stock trade value prediction has garnered significant attention from the academic and industry communities.
Global Recognition: His research papers have been published in internationally renowned journals indexed in Scopus, a testament to their quality and impact.
Fostering Knowledge Sharing: He has actively organized numerous events, including FDPs, workshops, and conferences, to promote knowledge sharing and collaboration among faculty and students.
We are immensely proud of Dr. Murali's accomplishments and his invaluable contributions to our institution. His dedication to research, teaching, and innovation inspires us all.

Dr M. Narendranadh Reddy

Dr M. Narendranadh Reddy, from the science and humanities department of Kits Engineering College, Vinjanampadu, has been bestowed with a doctorate degree by Kuppam Dravidian University. His research "A Study on MHD flow of Micro Polar Fluids with Radiation and Chemical Reaction" in the field of mathematics earned him this prestigious doctorate.
Speaking on the occasion, Chairman sri K. Subbarao expressed his pride in the achievement of Dr. M. Narendranadh Reddy and emphasized that among educators, continuous upskilling was necessary for rendering quality education to the learners.
He said earning a doctorate was a reflection of unmatched academic dedication and excellence. The present research addresses critical technical issues in the field of Mathematics.
It further reiterates the emphasis of the institution on academic and research excellence.
This achievement marks a milestone toward the college's goal of establishing an environment that promotes research and innovation.
The faculty and students are inspired by the accomplishments of Dr. Narendranadh Reddy in reinforcing the academic excellence image of the institution.
